About Rustic Acres

What started as a labor of love in 1991, baking for family gatherings and special occasions, grew into something bigger. By 1998, Cakes by Racheal Ruth was born, bringing homemade sweetness to the community for over two decades. In 2025, we rebranded as Rustic Acres Baked Creations, a fresh name with the same heart and passion behind every dessert. As a home bakery, we take pride in the personal touch that comes with every order, treating each creation like it's for our own family. From elegant wedding cakes to playful cake pops, custom cupcakes to holiday cookies, if you can dream it, we can bake it. No creation is too big or too small. Every dessert is made from scratch with care, creativity, and over 30 years of passion behind it.

*Rustic Acres operates under Missouri State Cottage Food Law.*
